Astrology is an ancient and intricate system that has captivated human curiosity for centuries. Central to astrology is the birth chart, a snapshot of the sky at the moment of an individual's birth. This chart serves as a roadmap, detailing the positions of the Sun, Moon, planets, and other celestial bodies in relation to the Earth and each other. Each element of the chart holds symbolic significance, representing different facets of a person's life and personality.
